# Appendix 1. Detailed Comparative Tables of Tax Features for Each Tax Category in African RECs

## Notes

1. Under **“Non-harmonized”**, the **“common/dominant rule”** represents the dominant rule across the relevant REC despite the lack of harmonization.
2. **Mostly-harmonized:** At most 1-member state (2 for CEN-SAD) deviates from the tax features.
3. **Partly-harmonized:** More than half of the member states adopt a similar approach to a tax feature.
4. **Non-harmonized:** Half or more of the member states adopt different rules to a tax feature.
